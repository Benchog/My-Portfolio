The Visionary Behind the Work

I am Patrick Benchog — a young entrepreneur, AI-powered builder, and problem solver based in Kumasi, Ghana. My tagline says it best: Built out of curiosity. I don't wait for someone to hand me a spec. I see a problem, and I build the solution.

I've built fully functional, production-ready apps and websites — real tools used by real people, not demos sitting in a folder. From a personal finance manager that gives complete clarity over your money, to a stock tracking system that eliminated weekly manual counting at my workplace, to a fee tool for non-citizens in Ghana that stops clients from travelling far only to arrive unprepared — every product I've built was born from a genuine pain point.

I work with AI as a force multiplier — taking ideas from concept to deployed product with speed and precision. I build web apps, Android PWAs, data dashboards, and business websites. I also bring CAD/CAM engineering skills (Solid Edge & AutoCAD), graphic design, and IT support to the table — making me one of the most versatile builders you'll find.

I'm not just adding tools to a resume. I'm building a body of work that speaks for itself. And I'm just getting started.

I don't wait for the perfect moment. I build the solution, ship it, and let the work do the talking.
